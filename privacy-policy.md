Last updated: June 21, 2026

StrPlay respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and the choices available to users of the StrPlay mobile application.

Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, StrPlay may collect or process:

* Email address
* User account identifier
* Authentication provider information
* Subscription and purchase status
* Subscription product and expiration information
* Device type and operating system
* Application version and language settings
* Application usage and interaction information
* Crash, diagnostic, and performance information
* Notification token, when notifications are enabled

Microphone and User Recordings

StrPlay requests microphone access only when you use recording features such as karaoke or duet recording.

The microphone is not used continuously. Recording starts only when you intentionally start a recording feature.

Karaoke and duet recordings may be stored locally on your device. These recordings are not shared with another person unless you choose to share or upload them using an available feature.

You can disable microphone permission at any time through your device settings. Some recording features may not function when microphone permission is disabled.

Music and Library Information

StrPlay may process or store information related to your use of the application, including:

* Favorite songs
* Liked songs
* Playlists

* Saved songs

* Karaoke and duet recording information
* Application preferences

Some information may be stored locally on your device. Account-related information may be synchronized with backend services where necessary.
