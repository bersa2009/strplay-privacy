# StrPlay Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

StrPlay respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect

- Email address
- Authentication information
- Subscription and purchase status
- Device and usage information
- Crash and performance diagnostics

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide premium subscription features
- Authenticate users
- Restore purchases
- Improve application stability and performance
- Prevent fraud and abuse

## Third-Party Services

StrPlay may use:

- Firebase Authentication
- Firebase Analytics
- Firebase Crashlytics
- Google Play Billing
- Apple In-App Purchases

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

## Subscription Information

Premium subscriptions are processed securely through Google Play and Apple App Store billing systems.

We do not store full payment card information on our servers.

## Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect user information.
